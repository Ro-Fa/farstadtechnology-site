About BiteFy

A simpler product check for allergy-aware shopping

BiteFy is designed to make it easier to check food products while shopping. The app helps users scan barcodes, review product information, and keep track of dietary preferences directly on their device.

BiteFy is intended as an informational support tool. Product information may come from third-party databases and may be incomplete or outdated. Users should always verify the packaging label before consuming a product.

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for BiteFy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

1. Overview

BiteFy is a mobile application that helps users scan food products, review ingredients, and check whether a product may fit their dietary preferences or allergy-related needs.

2. Information the app may use

BiteFy may use the following information and device features:

  • Camera access for scanning product barcodes.
  • Barcode numbers entered manually by the user.
  • Locally stored allergy, intolerance, or dietary preference settings.
  • Locally stored history and favorites created by the user.
  • Product information retrieved from third-party product databases or APIs.

3. Local storage

BiteFy may store certain user selections locally on the device, such as allergy settings, favorites, and scan history, in order to improve the user experience. This information is not intended to identify the user personally.

4. Third-party product data

BiteFy may retrieve product information from external sources. While we aim to present useful information, we do not guarantee that all product data is complete, accurate, or up to date.

5. Health and allergy disclaimer

BiteFy is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Users with allergies or severe intolerances should always verify the product packaging and ingredient label before use or consumption.

6. Data sharing

BiteFy is not designed to sell personal data. If product lookups are performed through third-party services, limited technical request data may be transmitted as part of normal app functionality.

7. Data retention

Locally stored information remains on the user’s device until it is removed by the user, deleted through app functionality, or removed when the app is uninstalled.
