About BiteFy

A simpler product check for allergy-aware shopping.

BiteFy is designed to make food product checks easier while shopping. The app helps users scan barcodes, review product information, and keep track of dietary preferences directly on their device.

BiteFy is intended as an informational support tool. Product information may come from third-party databases and may be incomplete or outdated. Users should always verify the packaging label before consuming a product.

Core principles

Clarity first

Reduce friction and make product checks easier to understand.

Built for everyday use

Quick enough to use in a store, simple enough to trust as a workflow.

Label verification matters

Packaging labels remain the final source of truth for consumption decisions.

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for BiteFy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Overview

BiteFy is a mobile application that helps users scan food products, review ingredients, and check whether a product may fit their dietary preferences or allergy-related needs.

2. Information the app may use

BiteFy may use camera access for barcode scanning, barcode numbers entered manually by the user, locally stored allergy or dietary settings, locally stored favorites and history, and product data retrieved from third-party sources.

3. Local storage

Certain settings and user-created data may be stored locally on the device to improve the app experience. This information is not intended to personally identify the user.

4. Third-party product data

BiteFy may retrieve product information from external sources. We do not guarantee that all product data is complete, accurate, or up to date.

5. Health and allergy disclaimer

BiteFy is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Users with allergies or severe intolerances should always verify the packaging and ingredient label before use or consumption.

6. Data sharing

BiteFy is not designed to sell personal data. If third-party services are used for product lookups, limited technical request data may be transmitted as part of normal functionality.

7. Data retention

Locally stored information remains on the user’s device until it is removed by the user, deleted through app functionality, or removed when the app is uninstalled.

8. Contact

For support or privacy-related questions, contact support@bitefy.no.
